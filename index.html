Our Apostolic Mission

We Love Serving Our Local Community

The Catholic Vicariate of Jimma Bonga operates deep humanitarian, educational, and developmental programs to fulfill both the spiritual mandate and social action goals of the Church.

Through the coordination of our Catholic Secretariat, we implement life-changing infrastructure and health programs to foster resilience in southwest Ethiopia.

Pastoral Services

Administering sacraments and expanding local faith groups across our 55 active parishes.

Youth Ministry

Nurturing and training the young generations with moral foundations, peace, and values.

Primary Education

Funding rural classroom blocks, supporting schools like the Obera project in Bonga.

Socio-Economic

Uplifting agrarian communities through agricultural empowerment and micro-credits.

Healthcare Desk

Operating local health posts to protect mothers, children, and vulnerable families.

Women's Promotion

Skill building, self-sufficiency training, and professional guidance for rural girls.

Vicar Apostolic

Bishop Markos
Ghebremedhin, C.M.

"The church in southwestern Ethiopia stands to empower both the body and soul of the community. In self-reliance and deep brotherhood, we serve."

Birth Place

Shappa – Kaffa Zone

Birth Date

18 November 1966

Ordained Priest

19 September 1993

Consecrated Bishop

24 January 2010

As a consecrated spiritual shepherd and Vice President of the **Catholic Bishops’ Conference of Ethiopia (CBCE)**, Bishop Markos works to foster a sustainable diocesan ecosystem. His vision coordinates pastoral networks, builds standard secondary schools, supports priestly welfare, and drives collaboration with overseas partners.
